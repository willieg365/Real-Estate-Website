Ready to Buy?

Contact Your Local REALTOR®

Now a days most buyers find it helpful to use a professional real estate broker to help guide them through the process. In fact in 2023 89% of home buyers used a real estate agent or broker to purchase their home. This REALTOR® will not only be able to help find them the right home, but is also going to help them negotiate, explain, and understand the real estate market. Typically, sellers fund the buyer's broker commission, which makes it cost-effective for the buyers. We understand that for most this is the biggest purchase in their life, and we are proud to help buyers achieve the American Dream!

Figure out how much home you can afford

Unless you are buying a home with all cash, getting pre-approved is the best way to see how much you can afford. Most sellers prefer a buyer with a pre-approval as opposed to a pre-qualified buyer. To get pre-approved contact your lender or ask me for my preferred lender.

Prioritize Needs

Now that you have a rough budget in mind it is time to make a list of must-have home features. Your price point most likely will dictate the location, size, and amenities of your home. Use as much detail as possible here to save time in the future.

Start your Home search

Searching for homes online can be exhausting. We will simplify the process by setting up a custom search tailored to your specific needs and updated real-time. You will have to try to stay flexible. Adjusting the search criteria as your home search continues is normal. Sometimes looking outside your search parameters may surprise you.
